Dig

Minezy is a tool for reporters who want to explore large email files. Whether you’re on deadline or a monthlong project, Minezy will help you quickly find and mine.

Analyse

Minezy allows you to figure out the five Ws in any email file, large or small: Who is emailing? What are they emailing? Why? What’s the subject? What’s the content. When were they sent?

Focus

Minezy helps you get to the most important players, the most important dates, the most important topics fast. It takes the guesswork out of massive email dumps, and helps you find the story.
