Dig

Minezy is a tool to explore large email archives. Whether you’re on deadline or a month-long project, Minezy will help you dig deeply and quickly to find and mine.

Analyze

Minezy allows you to figure out the five Ws in any email archive, large or small: Who emailed who? When did the emails occur? How often? Who else was involved? What was the subject and content?

Focus

Minezy helps you get to the most important players, the most important dates, the most important topics fast. It takes the guesswork out of massive email dumps, and helps you find the story.
